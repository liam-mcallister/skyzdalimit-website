Volunteering

Our brilliant volunteers are vital members of the Skyzdalimit team. They share our love of theatre and generously give their time to support our performances front-of-house.

How to Volunteer

Volunteers are a vital ingredient of our success. Our volunteers are greatly valued and celebrated and we truly appreciate the sacrifice they make in giving up their free time to help our cast.

We will do everything in our power to ensure your time with us will be enjoyable and that it will be a very positive and beneficial experience them.

Skyzdalimit is a recognised centre for the Millennium Volunteer Awards and we can nominate our volunteers to receive awards such as the MV200.

Some of the roles our volunteers typically perform during rehearsals are: assisting cast with dance routines, songs, music and acting; going over script with cast; setting up and putting away chairs, tables; serving water.

Some of the roles for the show production include: make-up; dressing-room costume assistant; stage-crew; stage-side prompters; on-stage assistants.
